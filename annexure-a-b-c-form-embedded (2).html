Annexure B – Other Items of Importance

Security Requirements

Each insurer has their own minimum security requirements. Failing to comply may result in rejection of a claim.

Construction Requirements

Standard construction includes brick, stone, concrete or metal framework and standard roofing materials. Other materials must be referred to insurer.

Policy Wording

The policy wording contains the terms, conditions, provisions, definitions and exceptions.

SASRIA

SASRIA provides cover against civil commotion, public disorder, strikes, riots and terrorism.

Excess Structures

Excess is the first amount payable. Types include Standard, Additional, Voluntary and Excess Waiver.

Average

Failure to insure property fully results in average (claims reduced proportionally).
